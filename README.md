#### "Beyond the Sound: The Geometry of Prana"

---

## SCOPE
A digital exploration of Tibetan typography and Yantra Yoga symbolism, where the letter serves as an energetic node rather than a mere phonetic marker. This project focuses on the procedural preservation of Tibetan graphemes as sacred geometries, investigating the layers of meaning embedded within the form—from the semantics of sound to the regulation of prana through visual meditation. It functions as a digital archive of "living script," where each vector contour is a projection of psychophysical states and ancient transmission lineages, revealing the silent power behind the syllable.

Using gradients in posters, flyers, and postcards with various letters and symbols. All files are vector-based and can be transformed to the desired size.
